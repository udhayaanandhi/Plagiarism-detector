Data is vital in every area of modern technology and innovation. As artificial intelligence grows, data analysis becomes more essential. Professionals require methods to evaluate structured data effectively. Normalization and data transformation help prepare inputs for learning models. Pattern detection enables systems to find irregularities quickly. In education, originality is key and plagiarism is a major concern. Algorithms like Rabin-Karp scan for repeating word sequences. N-gram based techniques can assess how similar two documents are. Similarity scoring tools aid in identifying copied or closely matched content. Academic honesty depends heavily on robust plagiarism detection systems. Many universities implement detection software to ensure fairness. Students should always reference the material they paraphrase or quote. Finite State Machines are used in text searching algorithms. Automata simplify the search process by minimizing unnecessary transitions. Aho-Corasick automaton allows for efficient multi-pattern matching in large texts.
